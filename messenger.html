Usability

The user interface of your application is the actual product you are selling.

Usability shouldn’t be applied as an afterthought. It’s crucial to product design, therefore must be considered from day one.

A good UI doesn’t make its user think. It solves problems, so its user doesn’t have to.

Easy to use. Easy to understand. Intuitive. Fast loading. Easy to maintain.
